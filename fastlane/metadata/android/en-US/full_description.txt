<i>Hisn Al-Muslim</i> is a calm Android app for reading, revisiting, and keeping close the daily adhkar from <i>Hisnul Muslim</i>.

Built for a focused daily reading experience, it brings together clear collection browsing, comfortable Arabic reading, favorites, and gentle reminders throughout the day.

<b>Features:</b>
- Calm collection-based home screen for browsing adhkar
- Swipeable dhikr reader for moving through each collection
- Arabic font selection and reading controls
- Transliteration, translation, and reference toggles
- Favorites and collection-only search
- Morning, evening, sleeping, and repeatable reminders
- Dynamic color, black theme, and appearance settings
- Works fully offline with a bundled dataset
